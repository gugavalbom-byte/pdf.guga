About NOTHING


On 16 October 2020, Carl Pei, the co-founder of OnePlus announced his resignation so that he could start a new venture.


Pei later raised up to $7 million from multiple investors to start up his venture, including Tony Fadell of iPod, Twitch co-founder KevinLin, Reddit CEO Steve Huffman, and YouTuber Casey Neistat.
